From DM's Desk

The district of Paschim Medinipur, carved out of erstwhile Medinipur district in 2002 is a vast, geographically diverse, socio culturally rich district wherein we try to deliver services to citizens amidst flood and drought, forests and plains, areas of bountiful agricultural productivity and despicable barrenness, for our people living in far flung villages with identified backwardness as well as to the cosmopolitan town of Kharagpur.
